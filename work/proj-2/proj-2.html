Cafe Maryli

Cafe Maryli was one of the earliest projects that I undertook. That was when I started learning about web development. Cafe Maryli was more of a passion project, A website for small buisness Cafes which would help the customer digitally navigate through the cafe by sharing reviews, keeping track of the menu and reserving tables or even a food item of their choice before hand. Cafe Maryli was designed to provide good customer service along with great food in a comfortable ambience.

Development

The "Homepage" and the "Menu" section was designed and developed for users throughout various platforms. The ergonomic design of the homepage eables the site to be ported on any device without much change in the layout of the page.Javascript has also been integrated to make the UX fluid and and the animation crisp.

Mobile Responsive

Cafe Maryli was also designed to run on mobile devices in the form of mobile website or mobile application therefore it was an obvious choice to make it mobile responsive for various phones and tablets.
