Kiwi Circular Sheet

Kiwi Circular Sheet was a the first project I undertook on a professional scale for a company called Kiwi. Kiwi is a Q&A app for iPhone and Android that is its own social network for asking and answering questions. The Circular which I was assigned to develop was the official circular for Kiwi Co. This project was handed to me by a Kiwi Co. Designer in Gurgoan.

Project Development

This Circular is developed using HTML5 and CSS3. The completion of the project took roughly 2 hours. The Circular is completely compatable for high resolution 1080p screens and completely scalable.

Mobile Responsive

I make a point to make all my designs mobile responsive for them to work on a wider range of plateforms. Thats the reason why I made this project mobile responsive despite of the circular being "desktop only".
